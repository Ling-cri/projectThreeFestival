Traditional Chinese acrobatic performances

12 :00 - 2:00

Chinese culture has a long history, and acrobatic art has always accompanied the development of culture. Modern acrobatics redesigns movements on the basis of inheriting traditional acrobatics techniques, making the use of props more comprehensive, increasing difficulty levels, diversifying forms of expression, and developing the potential of more acrobats. At the same time, modern acrobatics combines traditional Chinese opera performances with acrobatic tricks, and the stage forms are also more diverse.
