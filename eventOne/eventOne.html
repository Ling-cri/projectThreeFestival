The kick-off Event

High-pole Lion Dance

11 :00 - 11:30

Lion dance is a well-known folk activity that is traditionally believed to bring prosperity and good luck. Different from traditional lion dancing, high-pole lion dance has its own rules. Performers do not jump around on the ground but on poles ranging between one to three meters high. Besides, the space between poles also varies. The high-pole one is more challenging and dangerous.

Lacquer Fan

Parent-child handicraft Workshop

1 :00 - 2:00

Lacquer fans are traditional Chinese handicrafts. We will make lacquer fan combines traditional and modern creativity, using lacquer as a pen and water for painting, creating a mesmerizing interplay between human creativity and natural spontaneity. Each fan undergoes a unique transformation during its creation, resulting in a singular masterpiece that cannot be replicated.

Chinese shadow play

2:30-3:00

Chinese shadow play is an ancient traditional art form in Chinese folk culture. It uses flat, articulated puppets made of leather or paper to cast shadows on a screen. It is a form of storytelling and the use of light and shadow to bring tales to life.
