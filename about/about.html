About

Together in Tradition is a festival celebration event promoting traditional Chinese culture held at the Asian/Pacific American Heritage Month. This event lasts for three days and is held on the elementary school campus, mainly targeting the families of students in the school and people in the nearby community. The event aims to make Chinese American children proud of Chinese culture and to provide other ethnic families in the school and nearby neighborhood with a better understanding of Chinese culture.
