Together in Tradition is a festival celebration event promoting traditional Chinese culture held at the Asian/Pacific American Heritage Month. This event lasts for three days and is held on the elementary school campus, mainly targeting the families of students in the school and people in the nearby community. The event aims to make Chinese American children proud of Chinese culture and to provide other ethnic families in the school and nearby neighborhoods with a better understanding of Chinese culture.

The Joy Culture Foundation is a Chinese cultural non-profit organization. We want to provide a resource center and a platform for families with children (age 6 month to 12 years old) interested in Chinese literacy and culture. We aim to support diversity and become a Chinese cultural hub through childhood education in the Bay Area.
