Chinese food street

11 :00 - 4:00

This event showcases a variety of mouthwatering dishes from different regions of China, from sizzling stir friends and Savory dumping to sweet desserts and aromatic teas. In addition, there are also various traditional Chinese snacks that children enjoy.

sugar-coated haws

It's not just a delicacy, it also symbolizes sweetness and happiness. It is famous for its unique taste and various flavors. The outer layer of sugar coating is hard and crisp, making a crisp sound when bitten, while the fruit inside is sweet and juicy.

Sugar painting

It is a traditional folk handicraft, which takes sugar as the material for modeling. It has a beautiful and exquisite appearance, is crystal clear, and tastes crispy and delicious.

Shadow Play Workshop

1 :00 - 1:30

Shadow play is an ancient way of telling stories. It uses silhouette puppets to cast shadows onto the screen through light. Then, various effects are achieved by moving the puppet and light source. Children who watch shadow play on the first day can try making their own shadow puppetry in this workshop.
